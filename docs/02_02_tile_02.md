# Tile 2: Dense Residential

Tile 2 represents the first zone where proto-infrastructure begins to accumulate and coordinate spatially. Engineering solutions become more frequent and interrelated, but production-oriented or industrial systems remain absent.

- **Density:** Medium–High
- **Form:** Dense mid–high-rise residential (5–20 floors)
- **Atmosphere:** reflects the **lateral ambiance gradient** appropriate to this tile’s position and must not contradict the global progression
- **Key Elements**
	- Apartment blocks, 6–10 floor buildings, Active windows
	- Local commerce at street level
	- Older cars, buses
	- Tight urban grid
  - light coming from the right
- **Forbidden Motifs**
  - Iconic landmarks
  - Iconic skyline
  - Sun prohibited (see _Global Constraints / Lighting_).
  - Industrial structures
